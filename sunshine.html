Sunshine Ski Resort

Sunshine is located a short 20 minute drive from Banff, Alberta. When comparing proximity and resort significance this is the best resort to ski from Banff. Banff is the rockies greatest mountain town. Filled this busy streets and interesting shops this townsite is a must do. Therefore, a trip to Sunshine offers nice days of easy to mid level skiing mixed with days spent off the mountain in Banff. Sunshine therfore becomes a great option for those looking to ski with family.
